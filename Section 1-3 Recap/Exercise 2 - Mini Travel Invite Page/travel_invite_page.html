Trip to Shibuya

Shibuya's iconic role in Jujutsu Kaisen makes it a desirable destination. The series uses the real-world location as the backdrop for the "Shibuya Incident Arc." Familiar landmarks such as the Shibuya Crossing and Shibuya Station become the battleground for key moments in the story. Walking through the streets allows fans to visualize the battles between the sorcerers and cursed spirits. This provides a connection between the city and the series' supernatural world.
