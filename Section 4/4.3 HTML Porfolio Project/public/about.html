About Me

Hi, I’m Victor Acuña, a Computer Science and Engineering student at UCLA with a passion for blending creativity and technology. I enjoy designing intuitive, accessible, and visually engaging web experiences - whether that’s through crafting clean interfaces, building full-stack projects, or experimenting with game development in Unity.

I’ve worked on projects ranging from a responsive fashion web app that personalizes outfit recommendations to a top-down Unity game and a nonprofit initiative that teaches kids the basics of coding. My experiences as a Webmaster for MADE at UCLA and UI/UX Intern at DevX have helped me develop a strong eye for design and functionality.

Outside of coding, I love binge-watching shows, watching anime, listening to music, and exploring coffee shops - always with a matcha latte in hand. These small joys keep me inspired and remind me to approach every project with curiosity and creativity.
